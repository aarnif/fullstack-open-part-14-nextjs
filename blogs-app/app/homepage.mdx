# Blogs App

An blogs app created for the exercises in the [Full Stack Open Next.js course](https://courses.mooc.fi/org/uh-cs/courses/full-stack-open-nextjs).

## Features

- Sign up and log in to your own account.
- Share blog posts and explore individual posts in detail.
- Browse other users and see what they are reading.
- Save posts for later and mark them as read when you finish.

## Getting Started

1. [Sign up](/register) for an account or [log in](/login) if you already have one.
2. [Browse the latest blog posts](/blogs) and open any post to read more.
3. [Share your own blog posts](/blogs/new), like posts you enjoy, and save items for later.
4. Visit your [reading list](/me) to keep track of what you still want to read.

## Tech Stack

Built with Next.js, React, TypeScript, and a PostgreSQL database powered by Drizzle ORM.

View the [source code on GitHub](https://github.com/aarnif/fullstack-open-part-14-nextjs).
